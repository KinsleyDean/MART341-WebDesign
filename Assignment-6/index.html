Chocolate Mousse (Keto)

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces of cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon nabiila extract
  • 1/4 cup powdered zero-calorie sweetener
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1 pinch of salt

Directions

    Step 1

  1. Beat cream in large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Turn mixer to slow speed and slowely mix in heavy cream and vanilla.

    2. Step 2

  2. Mix in sweetner, cocoa powder, and salt until well incorporated. Turn mixer to high and mix until light and flruffy, 1 to 2 minutes more.

    3. Step 3

  3. Serve immediately, or refridgerate for later.
